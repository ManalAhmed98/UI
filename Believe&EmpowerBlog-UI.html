1.1 Personal Blog Meaning

A personal blog is an online platform on which you express your personal thoughts. Blogging gives you the freedom to express your ideas and share them with as many willing readers as possible. Honestly, the best feeling in the world is when you start seeing your readership grow.

You can write a personal blog on a wide variety of topics, or you can pick a niche. Picking a niche for your personal blog is, in my opinion, the best thing to do. Be careful not to choose a niche that’s too narrow. An example of a personal blog on a very narrow niche is “strawberry deserts.” A strawberry desert niche is too narrow because you are excluding chocolate deserts, vanilla deserts, etc as well as cakes (both design and how to make a cake) and a lot more. A more appropriate niche would be deserts. It’s narrow but it’s not too narrow.

In this blog, I write on personal development. Personal development covers a large area of topics from productivity, habits, entrepreneurship and online business and blogging. I think it fits me well because it allows me the freedom to write on the topics that I am passionate about without feeling too restricted.

If you want to start a personal blog on personal development, I would be happy to help you do just that. Feel free to use my blog as a model for yours.



1.2 Personal Blog Example

If you are still unsure what a personal blog is, here is an example of one that I recently came across.

It’s called Brute Reason .

The author of Brute Reason talks about social justice issues. The blog combines a mix of personal views, contemporary issues, gender and a lot more. Check it out if you want to get another feel for what a personal blog is all about.

You too can start a personal blog on a topic that you care about and share the message with your readers. The great thing about starting a personal blog is that you can decide just how personal you want it to be. For example, you can start a personal blog to sell your own personal brand, or to promote awareness regarding topics that are near and dear to your heart, or even to document a certain personal journey – of starting a business, beating an illness, losing weight, etc.

There is really no limit to what to write your personal blog about.

2. How to Start a Personal Blog

To start a blog, you need two things:

A hosting provider + blogging platform.

Your hosting provider will be the company with which you will “host” your blog. It will be on their servers.

Your blogging platform will be your content management system (CMS). This is the place where you get to determine what goes into your blog – in terms of posts, pages, images and videos.
